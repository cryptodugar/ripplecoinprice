Our Motto
ripplecoinprice.org aims to provide fast loading accurate Ripple coin price charts in every national currency. We are neutral on Ripple and merely present the price of Ripple. We do not have any opinion for or against Ripple. We do not buy Ripple and we do not sell Ripple. We simply present numbers, for which Ripple is traded in national currencies around the world every second of the day. Over the coming months ripplecoinprice.org will release a Ripple Price iPhone and Ripple Price Android app, as well as Ripple Price charts for your website in over 140 national currencies. So you can place live Ripple price charts on your own website for free in any national currency in the world. We will also be releasing the best live Ripple Price charts in the industry.
